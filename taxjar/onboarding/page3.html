Analysis

The Good
Sign up page is clear in presenting that this is a free trial - no ambiguity there. It's super simple to get started, just provide an email and choose a password. There is a clear call to action button that is very clickable.

Nitpicks
There is no way for me to visualize using the product (no screenshots, testimonials, videos). Also, it is not clear why I should sign up. How will this product help me solve my problems - what will it do for me? As a user who is about to hand over my financial data, what guarantees do you provide? Uptime, security, and ownership of data all are concerns in the back of my head.

Proposition

Below is a simple HTML mockup of a possible alternative sign up page. It features a clear value prop with some supporting text. I've included a placeholder that will hold a screenshot of the product. The signup form has been expanded out to ask for a first / last name that will be used to add some more personal greetings later on in the flow. At the bottom is a simple FAQ that answers questions about security, data integrity, payment options and trial expiration.
