Analysis

The Good
Super simple sign up process. Clicked Signup and was in the app immediately. From this page connecting my data is simple, I just have to click on the buttons and follow any instructions. There's plenty of options and even one for CSV uploading. The transparent background gives me clues / hints as to what I can expect.

Nitpicks
The gray pop up looks and feels just like a "hey there" welcome message, so I didn't bother reading it and just tried to close it. There's quite a bit of available navigation on the page - it does clue me into what I can expect to do - but I can't make use of any it. It is a little distracting.

Proposition

Make the transition from signup to application ready a little more smooth. I would propose a simple wizard to guide the user through several key actions.

1. Connecting their account to a cart.
2. Updating Business Profile. (skipable)
3. Quick guide / tutorial. (skipable)
